Chicken 65

  • Time: 45 mins
  • Serves: family
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 1 kg
kashmiri Chilly 5 tbspn
Chilli Powder 2 spn
garam masala 2 spn
black pepper 2 spn
oil 100 ml
ginger 2 tbspn
garlic 3 pcs

Step 1: Marination - chicken left to marinate for few hours is important. The longer you marinate the juicy the chicken gets. marinate the chicken for at least 1 to 2 hours upto overnight.

Step 2: mix all other incrediants together.Lemon juice, baking powder and yogurt added in the chicken makes it so tender and juicy. It breaks down the tough meat and makes it so succulent.

Step 3: Drop in hot oil and fry till golden. It will take around 7 to 10 mins depending on the size of the chicken.

Step 4: Drain it and serve hot with some fried curry leaves and lemon wedges

Chicken Roast

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: family
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken with Skin 1.5 kg
Butter 1/2 cup
Coriander Powder 1 spn
Turmeric Powder 1 spn
garlic 2 pcs
Cumin Powder 2 spn
Chilli Powder 2 spn
garam masala 2 spn
black pepper 2 spn

Step 1: Take chicken in a baking pan and use a kinfe to poke holes all through the chicken. Take butter in a sauce pan and melt it down. Now add all the ingredients into it and mix well.

Step 2: Now spoon the marination over the chicken and massage really well..Spoon marination into the cavity as well. Put the chicken in fridge and let it marinate for 2 hours, you can leave to marinate overnight as well

Step 3: Take the chicken out of fridge 30 mins or 1 hour before baking. Meanwhile preheat oven to 180 degree C.Cover the tray with foil and pop the chicken in oven and bake for 45 mins. Now remove it and spoon some of the juices from the tray and cover with foil again and pop into the oven and bake for another 45 mins.

Step 4: Now remove the chicken from oven, increase the heat of the oven to 220 degree C. Remove the foil from the tray, spoon the juice collected in the tray over the chicken and bake for another 35 to 40 mins, spoon the juices over the chicken every 10 mins.

Step 5: Remove the chicken from oven and let it rest for 15 to 30 mins. If you have any juices in the tray just spoon it over the chicken. This will keep the chicken moist.Sprinkle over some coriander leaves and serve.

Crumped Chicken Fry

  • Time:15 mins
  • Serves:family
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 1 kg
Breadcrumbs 2 cups
Chilli Powder 2 spn
garam masala 2 spn
black pepper 2 spn
egg 2 pcs
lemonr 2 tbspn
cummin powder 3 spn

Step 1: Take buttermilk in a bowl and add all the marinate ingredients. Mix well.

Step 2: Add the drumsticks in this and mix well. Let this marinate for 30 mins.

Step 4: Now take breadcrumbs in a bowl. Take each drumsticks and coat them with breadcrumbs.

Step 5: Arrange this on a rack and leave it for 5 to 10 mins so it gets dry and the coating sticks to the chicken.

Step 6: Now heat oil for deep frying. When it is hot, drop the chicken and fry on a medium heat for 10 mins on each side. Flip over and cook on other side too.Dont fry on a high heat, because then the outer will get over browned, but interior will be raw. So always fry on a low to medium heat.

Step 7: When it is cooked drain on a paper towel.Serve with ketchup or mayo.
